ESIndigo

ESIndigo is a project fair extending over two successive days and which aims to share and sell ideas and personal projects made by students. This event is intended not only for this category of students but also for companies that need projects to invest in, and for other students to motivate and inspire themselves. Participants will also be able to experience teamwork around a project that meets the needs of a company. A new concept will be introduced by this first edition of ESIndigo which is “code & sell”.
